
# Jaco Electric — Facebook Ad Landing Page

A high-converting landing page designed to turn Facebook ad traffic into leads (calls + form submissions) for Jaco Electric in the Tulsa/Broken Arrow area.

## Brand Colors
- **Primary**: Dark charcoal/navy (#2D3436) matching their site header
- **Accent**: Yellow/gold (#F1C40F) from their logo
- **White** for text contrast and clean sections

---

## Page Sections (top to bottom)

### 1. Hero Section — Attention + Offer
- Bold headline: **"Tulsa's Most Trusted Electricians — Get $50 Off Your First Service"**
- Subheadline reinforcing trust: "Licensed (OK #094652) · 30+ Years Experience · Free Estimates"
- Prominent **click-to-call button** with phone number (918) 894-1622
- A secondary CTA button: "Get Your Free Quote" (scrolls to form)
- Service area mention: Broken Arrow, Tulsa, Bixby, Jenks & surrounding areas

### 2. Services Overview — Quick Visual Grid
- 3 service cards with icons:
  - **Residential Service** — repairs, breakers, outlets, lighting
  - **New Construction & Remodels** — full electrical wiring
  - **Panel Upgrades & Inspections** — code compliance, safety
- Each card is brief (2 lines max) to keep the page scannable

### 3. Trust & Social Proof
- "Why Choose Jaco Electric?" with 3-4 trust badges:
  - ⚡ Licensed & Insured
  - ⭐ 30+ Years Combined Experience
  - 💰 95% of Jobs Billed at Estimate
  - ✅ Free Estimates, No Surprises
- 3 customer testimonials (Brian Palmer, Linda Birchall, Cheri Hutson) displayed as cards with star ratings

### 4. Lead Capture Form — The Conversion Point
- Headline: **"Claim Your $50 Discount — Get a Free Quote Today"**
- Short form fields: Name, Phone, Email, Service Needed (dropdown), Brief Description (optional)
- Big yellow CTA button: "Request My Free Quote"
- Below form: "Or call us now" with click-to-call link
- Trust note: "We respond within 1 hour during business hours"

### 5. Sticky Mobile CTA Bar
- Fixed bottom bar on mobile with "Call Now" and "Get Quote" buttons
- Always visible as users scroll — maximizes conversion on mobile (where most Facebook traffic lands)

---

## Design & Conversion Principles
- **Mobile-first** — 80%+ of Facebook ad traffic is mobile
- **Fast-loading** — no heavy images, optimized for speed
- **Single-page, no navigation** — eliminate distractions, keep users focused on converting
- **Urgency** — limited-time discount messaging
- **Trust signals throughout** — license number, testimonials, experience stats
- All data stays client-side (no backend needed) — form submissions show a success message with the phone number to call
